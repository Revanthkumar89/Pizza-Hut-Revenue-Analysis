# Pizza-Hut-Revenue-Analysis
The Pizza Sales Dashboard is a data visualization project designed to analyze pizza sales trends and customer preferences.
It provides insights into monthly sales performance, busiest days, peak ordering hours, preferred pizza categories, and sizes.
This dashboard helps businesses make data-driven decisions related to staffing, inventory, and marketing.
Key Insights:
   Certain days of the week generate significantly higher orders
   Evening hours show peak demand
   Veggie pizzas dominate customer preferences
   Medium and Large sizes are most frequently ordered
Business Impact:
   Improves demand forecasting
   Helps optimize staffing during peak hours
   Assists in inventory and supply planning
   Supports targeted marketing campaigns
